Hack Nights for the Twin Cities.

Hack on your own projects or help others with theirs.

Unlike other meetup groups in town, we do not organize talks.
Check out the good folks at Ruby MN, Cocoaheads, or the Google Developer Group for that.

  • Mobile Hack

    First Wednesday of the month
    6-8pm

  • Rails Hack

    Third Thursday of the month
    6-8pm

4 Principles

  • Everyone is welcome

    All skill levels.

  • No speakers or talks

    No talks, no agenda, no program.

  • Good beer and grub

    Pizza + MN craft beer.

  • Open Source focus

    Recommended, not required.

For the community, by the community
